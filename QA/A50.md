## Decision

Proceed with ADR-69 using local upstream clone diffs and scoped pytest
reruns as the non-LLM semantic evidence. Do not use `PAT_GITHUB` for
this block because the three claims are code/test-grounded and do not
depend on PR comments, reviews, or private data.

## Binding acceptance standard for ADR-69

For each reviewed case, record:

- case id and replay directory;
- seed claim id, claim type, claim text, and partition;
- upstream repo URL, PR number, base SHA, head SHA;
- inspected diff summary;
- exact scoped pytest rerun command, exit code, and terminal summary;
- pass2/grounded-report semantic alignment notes;
- residual uncertainty.

Allowed outcomes are:

- `manual_semantic_pass`
- `manual_semantic_fail`
- `ambiguous_insufficient_evidence`

G-6a may close only if all three current load-bearing archived replay
cases pass. Closure is limited to this archived replay set; future
LLM-authored replay sets must inherit static-plus-manual review before
their replay content carries claim-supporting weight.
